Workouts by Plan

These are simple example templates. The idea is consistency, not perfection.

Starter Plan Workouts

3× per week full-body, 45 minutes per session.

  • Day A: Squats, push-ups (or wall push-ups), dumbbell row, glute bridge, plank
  • Day B: Lunges, incline push-ups, hip hinge (light deadlift or kettlebell), shoulder press, side plank
  • Finish each session with 5–10 mins easy walking or cycling.

Fat Loss Focus Workouts

4× per week with a mix of strength and circuits.

  • Day 1 – Upper: Bench press, row, shoulder press, lat pulldown, curls
  • Day 2 – Lower: Squats, Romanian deadlifts, leg press, calf raises
  • Day 3 – Circuits: 30s work / 30s rest – bodyweight squats, push-ups, rows, mountain climbers, kettlebell swings
  • Day 4 – Full Body: Mix of lighter versions of Day 1 + 2

Muscle Gain Workouts

Push / Pull / Legs style split aimed at progression.

  • Push: Bench press, incline press, overhead press, lateral raises, triceps dips/extensions
  • Pull: Deadlifts or rack pulls, pull-ups/lat pulldowns, rows, face pulls, biceps curls
  • Legs: Squats, leg press, lunges, hamstring curls, calves
  • Repeat with variations for 4–5 days per week total.
