Jade’s Starter Plan Journey

Jade works a full-time job and used to restart a “new” workout every Monday. With the Starter Plan she committed to just three full-body sessions per week and short walks on the other days.

She focused on consistency instead of perfection. After three months she was stronger, sleeping better, and finally felt like exercise was part of her routine instead of a punishment.

“Three months of consistency and I feel like a different person.”

Andre’s Fat Loss Focus

Andre didn’t want extreme diets or two-hour gym sessions. The Fat Loss Focus plan gave him a clear schedule: four days of strength-and-cardio training with simple food guidelines he could follow around his work day.

By tracking his steps, lifting regularly, and sticking to his calorie targets, he dropped body fat while keeping his strength.

“The workouts were simple enough to follow after work. No crazy 2-hour sessions, just solid training.”

Leah’s Muscle Gain & Meal Reset

Leah wanted to get stronger without giving up family dinners. The Muscle Gain plan focused on progressive strength training three times per week and simple, higher-protein meals she could cook at home.

Instead of a strict diet, she used a few key “anchor meals” she could repeat each week and learned how to adjust portion sizes on busy days.

“The meal ideas were realistic. I didn’t have to give up everything—just be a lot more intentional.”
