Nutrition Basics

You don’t need a perfect diet. You just need something you can actually stick to that matches your training plan.

Starter Plan – Sample Day

  • Breakfast: Oats with milk, banana, peanut butter
  • Lunch: Rice, chicken, mixed veggies
  • Snack: Greek yogurt with fruit
  • Dinner: Pasta with ground beef and salad

Fat Loss Focus – Sample Day

  • Breakfast: Eggs, wholegrain toast, fruit
  • Lunch: Chicken salad with light dressing
  • Snack: Apple & a handful of nuts
  • Dinner: Fish, potatoes, vegetables

Muscle Gain – Sample Day

  • Breakfast: Oats with milk, berries & protein shake
  • Lunch: Rice, chicken or beef, vegetables
  • Snack: Wrap or sandwich with lean meat & cheese
  • Dinner: Pasta or potatoes, meat, veg, plus a dessert like yogurt or fruit

These are just examples – not strict meal plans. The main idea: keep protein in each meal, choose mostly whole foods, and match your calories to your goal.
