Pick the style that matches your lifestyle. No “12-week shred or you’re a failure” energy here – just simple plans you can actually stick to.

How to Choose a Plan

  1. New or returning to training? Start with the Starter Plan for 4–6 weeks.
  2. Goal is mainly fat loss? Choose Fat Loss Focus and keep steps + sleep in check.
  3. Already comfortable in the gym? Go straight to Muscle Gain and track your lifts.
  4. Not sure? Start with Starter, then move to Fat Loss or Muscle Gain depending on how you feel.

Sample Weekly Schedule

Plan Days Example Split
Starter 3 days Mon – Wed – Fri full-body
Fat Loss 4 days Upper / Lower / Cardio-circuits / Full-body
Muscle Gain 5–6 days Push / Pull / Legs / Upper / Lower (+ optional arms)

Rest days still count as part of the plan. Use them for light walking, stretching and not hating your life.
